⚠ Disclaimer: Kindly note that these are youtubers which I personally used during my preparation.

1. Vedantu: Vedantu is an Indian online tutoring platform where teachers provide tuitions to students over the internet, using a real-time virtual learning environment named WAVE. It operates on a marketplace model for teachers, where students can browse, discover and
choose to learn from an online tutor.

Visit their youtube channel



2. Edumantra: Through their tutorial programmes students get to know their subjects with more clarity and extent. They also prepare the students abilities to write their examinations as per the desired standards. Best facilities are provided to enable and enhance students potentials.
Visit their youtube channel


3. Bhai Ki Padhai: Their crisp and comprehensive video sessions teach you in a quick go without making the lesson extensive and dull. With more than 5,000 lessons in all major subjects for class 6th-12th, they provide explanatory tutorials to the learners. Because e-learning
content is interactive and leaves a long lasting impression on your mind us.

Visit their youtube channel



4. Dont Memorise: The vision of the Don't Memorise team is to help students master life. They want to show you the simplest way to understand a topic through our bite-sized, and engaging video lessons and hope to get you excited about learning and knowing more.

Visit their youtube channel




5. Shobhit Nirwan: Shobhit Nirwan from Delhi, currently pursuing B.Tech. from Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT)
Vadodara in IT Branch. His aim is to selflessly help each individual by providing free and quality education so that even unprivileged can achieve their full potential.

Visit their youtube channel



6. Atharva Puranik: Here you will get all the tips about Class 10 Maths, Science and how you can be amongst the top scorers! Apart from that, you will be able to learn new skills being in high school. With a vision of getting financially independent at a young age, He wants each and every student to know the power of getting financially Independent.

Visit their youtube channel
